THE STORY SO FAR

I create beautiful, usable, professional websites using best practice accessibility and the latest W3C web standards guidelines, resulting in semantic and seo friendly XHTML and CSS. All my websites are lovingly hand coded. For building websites my weapons of choice are pencil and paper, then Photoshop(overall design) and Fireworks(wireframe) and/or illustrator for creating text and font layouts, then Sublime paired with a combination of grunt/gulp/webpack to handle sass and whatever javascript libraries I'm using. Usually this is my work pipe My CMS of choice are Expression Engine and Wordpress Currently i'm teaching myself, Objective-c and Cocoa Touch for iPhone app development. I've released one series of iPhone apps: iStreetDance – iPhone apps for learning street dance. I'm a keen user of Cinema 4D and ZBrush and use them whenever I have the chance to add any 3D elements to my projects. When I'm not in front of my computer designing, you'll find me in front of my computer(!) practicing my digital drawing, and ZBrush. I live in Tunbridge wells, Kent, with my wife and my Jack Russell – Gubbit. I love to travel, and I love my job! I am currently available for freelance work.

WHO AM I?

My name is Ismael Rivera. I'm 37 and people on the english speaking spectrum tend to call me Ish because well... they just do. I'm currently a freelance creative designer/developer based in Porto Alegre, Brasil who used to run his own shop as dev/ops officer for a couple of non-profits in Rio Grande do Sul. Way before that, I was lead creative artist for Passman Galleries in Miami where I was responsible for coordinating projects between Nevada and Cayman Island branches. Some of my work was featured in high class magazines such as: VEGAS, Islands, YACHTS INTERNATIONAL and Robb Report. During which I also co-founded Speggo Designs, a small web design firm which I still run today and manage in tangent with an app component testing module PaaS which I still work on and use for my projects.

Soon to be moving back to Puerto Rico, my homeland. My specialty still lies in websites, particularly WORDPRESS, however, 3D visualisation, identity and dev/ops are huge components of my work. I also ghost write on occasion for copywrithers and bloggers. I dig reading and writing in all aspects but my favorite subjects by far involve nature, history, music, technology, design, science and conciousness(which could just as well encompass spiritualism and philosophy just as much as any other subject.

WHAT I DO

I create beautiful, usable, professional websites using best practice accessibility and the latest W3C web standards guidelines, resulting in semantic and seo friendly XHTML and CSS. All my websites are lovingly hand coded. For building websites my weapons of choice are pencil and paper, then Photoshop(overall design) and Fireworks(wireframe) and/or illustrator for creating text and font layouts, then Sublime paired with a combination of grunt/gulp/webpack to handle sass and whatever javascript libraries I'm using. Usually this is my work pipe My CMS of choice are Expression Engine and Wordpress Currently i'm teaching myself, Objective-c and Cocoa Touch for iPhone app development. I've released one series of iPhone apps: iStreetDance – iPhone apps for learning street dance. I'm a keen user of Cinema 4D and ZBrush and use them whenever I have the chance to add any 3D elements to my projects. When I'm not in front of my computer designing, you'll find me in front of my computer(!) practicing my digital drawing, and ZBrush. I live in Tunbridge wells, Kent, with my wife and my Jack Russell – Gubbit. I love to travel, and I love my job! I am currently available for freelance work.
